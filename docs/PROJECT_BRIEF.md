# AI Character Vlogger Platform - Project Brief

Date: 2026-05-04

## One-Line Concept

A creator platform that lets anyone run a vlog channel through a consistent AI character, without appearing on camera or managing the full production workflow manually.

## Product Positioning

This is not just an AI video generator. The product should be positioned as an operating system for AI character vlog creators:

- Character IP creation
- Observation-entertainment-style vlog planning
- Short-form video production
- Multi-platform publishing
- Fan relationship and monetization management

The sharper positioning is:

> Create and operate your own AI character vlogger, then publish optimized vlog content to Shorts, TikTok, Reels, and paid fan platforms.

## Core Insight

Many people want to create content but hesitate because of face exposure, filming burden, editing work, voice exposure, and inconsistent posting. AI character vlog content lowers those barriers while keeping the creator in control of concept, story, and monetization.

For AI character vlog, the key is not photorealism alone. It is character continuity:

- Same identity
- Same visual style
- Same voice and speech habits
- Same recurring routines
- Same relationship with fans
- Clear disclosure that the creator is AI/virtual

## Target Users

Initial target users should be narrow:

- People who want to build a vlog channel without showing their face
- AI influencer and virtual character operators
- Creators who want to connect public short-form content to Fanvue, Patreon, or membership channels
- Small brands that want a mascot-style vlogger
- Webtoon, game, or virtual idol teams that want to turn IP into short-form content

Avoid starting with "everyone." The first wedge should be AI character creators who care about repeatable content and monetization.

## Content Direction

The content format should resemble a mix of:

- TV observation entertainment: daily routine, personality, small reactions, recurring behavior
- Short-form influencer content: fast hook, tight edits, frequent publishing
- Character IP: lore, style, fan attachment, repeatable world
- Fan platform content: deeper interaction, paid extras, behind-the-scenes content

Recommended public channel format:

- 15-45 second SFW vlog clips
- Daily routines, style/fashion, room tour, Q&A, fan polls, small missions
- Published to YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels, and similar platforms

Recommended paid channel format:

- Longer vlog cuts
- Behind-the-scenes character content
- Personalized fan interaction
- PPV episodes or premium posts

## Product Thesis

The winning product is not a tool that creates one good video. It is a platform that helps creators operate a character channel over time.

That requires:

- Character bible and persona versioning
- Episode planning
- Script and scene generation
- Visual and voice consistency
- Platform-specific packaging
- Publishing workflow
- Analytics and feedback loops
- Fan memory and monetization support

## Strategic Recommendation

Start as a creator-side workflow product, not a consumer social network.

The near-term product should help creators generate and distribute AI character vlog content to existing platforms where discovery already exists. Building a standalone viewer platform can come later only if the creator tool gains traction.
