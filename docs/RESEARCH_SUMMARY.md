# Research Summary

## AI Image And Video Generation In Industry

AI image generation is already used in:

- Advertising and campaign creative
- Ecommerce product imagery
- Fashion virtual try-on and virtual models
- Creator tools and design workflows
- Film, game, and concept art production
- Architecture and interior visualization
- Synthetic data for healthcare, robotics, autonomous driving, and industrial AI

The major industrial pattern is not only generating new images. It is generating, adapting, localizing, testing, and publishing many variants quickly.

## AI Influencers And Fan Platforms

AI influencers are used as virtual brand characters, campaign models, AI-generated UGC-style ads, virtual fandom properties, and personalized fan-facing characters.

Fanvue is more AI-creator-friendly than OnlyFans in positioning. It supports AI creators, but requires disclosure and prohibits harmful synthetic media such as non-consensual deepfakes or underage-coded AI characters.

## Fanvue API Findings

Fanvue API supports:

- OAuth 2.0 with PKCE
- Chats and messages
- Mass messages
- Templates
- Custom and smart lists
- Media upload
- Vault folders
- Posts and scheduled posts
- Insights and earnings data
- Webhooks for messages, followers, subscriptions, purchases, and tips
- Agency-style creator management endpoints

It does not appear to provide a public "character persona API." Persona, character memory, and content rules should be stored outside Fanvue and connected to Fanvue through messages, posts, media, and webhooks.

## Vlog Platform Findings

Relevant platforms for AI character vlog distribution:

- YouTube Shorts
- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- Facebook Reels
- Snapchat Spotlight
- Pinterest Video Pins
- X
- Threads
- LinkedIn for B2B creator/process content
- Vimeo for hosting/portfolio

For MVP, prioritize:

1. YouTube Shorts
2. TikTok
3. Instagram Reels

The product should begin with export-ready publishing packages, then add automatic publishing after platform integration and app review constraints are handled.

## Observation Entertainment Connection

AI character vlog resembles TV observation entertainment because both rely on:

- Daily routine
- Character habits
- Small reactions
- Repeated formats
- Emotional attachment over time

The difference is that observation entertainment depends on real people and broadcast production, while AI character vlog depends on designed character continuity and fast interactive feedback from fans.

Useful content grammar:

- Fixed routines
- Small missions
- Character reactions
- Fan interaction
- Captions and narration
- Season arcs

## Strategic Conclusion

The product should be built as an AI character creator operating platform, not a generic video generator.

Core strategic stack:

```text
Character IP creation
+ Observation-style vlog planning
+ Short-form production automation
+ Multi-platform publishing
+ Fan relationship and monetization management
```
